# Salesforce Banking CRM Simulation

## Business Problem

A banking organisation required a structured CRM solution to manage customer relationships, service cases, activities, and internal access while improving visibility across customer interactions.

## Solution

A Salesforce CRM environment was configured to centralise customer information, support case management, automate business processes, and provide reporting visibility.

## Key Configuration

- Accounts and Contacts for customer relationship management
- Case management and lifecycle tracking
- Record-triggered Salesforce Flow automation
- Security and access controls
- Role hierarchy, permission sets, and sharing rules
- Reports and dashboards for operational visibility

## Business Analysis Approach

The solution was developed using business requirements, process analysis, system configuration, testing, and documented evidence to demonstrate how Salesforce can support real-world banking CRM operations.

## Outcome

The solution demonstrates improved customer information visibility, structured case handling, workflow automation, controlled system access, and management reporting.
