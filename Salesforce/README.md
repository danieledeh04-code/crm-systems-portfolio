# Salesforce Portfolio

## Overview

This section of my portfolio demonstrates practical Salesforce CRM solutions developed using realistic business scenarios. The projects showcase CRM configuration, security, automation, reporting, data management, and business process improvement.

The aim is to demonstrate how Salesforce can be used to solve business problems while applying Business Analysis and Salesforce Administration best practices.

## Projects Included

- Banking CRM Simulation
- Finance Application Decisioning
- Housing Community Campaigns
- Music Campaign Management
- Security & Access Management
- SLA & Entitlement Management
- List Views & Navigation
